Bulge Busters!

Place your towers to help defend your community against junkfood.

  • Click anywhere on the map to place a tower.
  • The towers will automattically find the junkfood creeps and shoot.
  • Place more towers to kill more creeps faster.
  • The creeps get more and more difficult as time goes on.
  • Good luck, have fun, but most importantly....put down that twinkie!
